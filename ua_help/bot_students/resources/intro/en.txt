The project is aimed at helping schoolchildren from Ukraine who are forced to interrupt their education. We will try to attract volunteers to conduct free online classes (mainly from Russia).

Main page [https://www.facebook.com/edu.help.for.ua/](www.facebook.com/edu.help.for.ua)

The project is available in the tg-channel [https://t.me/edu_ukraine](t.me/edu_ukraine)

We are currently gathering information about student needs. Depending on demand, we will develop the project in one direction or another. The project is crude, we do not promise any results yet, but at least we will try to provide information support
